Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or any data beyond what is necessary to operate the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide you with the expense tracking service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and allowing you to filter and manage your records. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app for your benefit.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers and is associated with your account. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all your personal data and expense records from our systems. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use trusted infrastructure providers to host the application, but these providers do not have access to your expense data beyond what is required to operate the servers. Any third-party services used are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us through the support information available within the app. We are committed to addressing any privacy concerns promptly and transparently.
